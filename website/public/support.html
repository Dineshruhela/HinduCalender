Frequently Asked Questions

Is The Hindu Calendar free?
Yes, the app is completely free to download and use. It is supported by ads, which help keep all features accessible at no cost.
Which platforms is the app available on?

What is Panchang?
Panchang (पंचांग) is the traditional Hindu almanac. It covers five elements of the day: Tithi (lunar day), Nakshatra (lunar mansion), Yoga, Karana, and Var (weekday). Our app shows today's Panchang with sunrise, sunset, and moonrise times.
How do I find Muhurta for an event?
Open the app's Home tab and scroll to the Muhurta section. It lists today's auspicious time windows (Abhijit, Brahma Muhurta, etc.). Tap any entry for a detailed explanation.
What is Choghadiya?
Choghadiya (चौघड़िया) divides each day and night into eight time segments, each ruled by a planet and rated as auspicious (शुभ), inauspicious (अशुभ), or neutral. The app shows both day and night Choghadiya for each day.
How do I generate a Kundli (birth chart)?
Tap the Kundli tab at the bottom. Enter the birth date, time, and place of birth. The app will generate a Vedic birth chart with planetary positions. For Kundli Milan, switch to the Milan sub-tab and enter details for both individuals.
The app is showing ads — can I remove them?
Ads are how we keep the app free for everyone. There is no paid/premium plan currently. If ads are disrupting your experience, please contact us and we'll look into it.
The app is crashing or not loading. What should I do?
Please try the following steps:
  1. Close and reopen the app.
  2. Restart your device.
  3. Check for an app update in the App Store / Play Store.
  4. Uninstall and reinstall the app (this does not delete any data).
If the problem persists, please email us with your device model and OS version.
The festival dates seem incorrect. How are they calculated?
Festival dates are calculated according to the traditional Hindu lunisolar calendar. Some regional variations exist (e.g. North vs. South India). If you notice a discrepancy, please email us with the festival name and the date you expected — we appreciate the feedback.
How do I request a new feature or report a bug?
We'd love to hear from you! Email us at dinesh.jony@gmail.com with a description of the feature or bug. You can also leave a review on the App Store or Play Store.
