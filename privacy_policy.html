Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Dinesh Ruhela ("we", "our", or "us") operates the mobile application The Hindu Calender (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Our App is designed to be privacy-conscious. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personal information like your name, email, or phone number directly.

Data Collected Automatically (Third-Party Services)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the App to keep it free. AdMob may collect and process certain information from your device, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, and unique device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID / IDFA).
  • Location Data: Coarse location data (such as country or city) based on your IP address to deliver relevant ads.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions with the ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

The automatically collected information is used exclusively to:

  • Serve appropriate contextual or personalized advertisements (via Google AdMob).
  • Analyze general usage trends to improve the App's performance and fix bugs.

3. Managing Your Data and Opting Out

iOS Devices: You can opt out of personalized advertising by going to Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking and turning off "Allow Apps to Request to Track" for The Hindu Calender.

For more information on how Google uses your data for advertising, please visit the Google Privacy & Terms page.

4. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can take necessary actions.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
